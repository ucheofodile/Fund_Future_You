Fund Future You
The Money Framework
You're not bad with money. You're built to protect it.
Picture the version of you five years from now. What does her life look like? Everything below is about funding her.
01
THE THREE THIRDS RULE
Every time money comes in, divide it into three equal parts before you spend a single naira or dollar.
THE ESSENTIALS THIRD THE SECURITY THIRD THE FUTURE THIRD
Day-to-day expensesSecurity & flex spendingSavings — do not touch
Transport, food, rent, utilities, subscriptionsGifts, emergencies, unexpected costs, small treatsThis is future you. Automate it. Forget it. Let it grow.
The Emotional Economy: The Security Third is also where the invisible costs live — the cough syrup, the birthday gift, the small thing nobody asked for but somebody needed. These costs are real. They belong here. Spending on them is not waste. It is care — funded properly.
Live your entire life on the Essentials Third. If you need it, the Security Third is there — not the Future Third.
02
SPLIT YOUR SAVINGS INTO THREE PORTFOLIOS
The Future Third is not one account. Split it into three buckets based on your goals and risk appetite.
PORTFOLIOPURPOSEEXAMPLESRISK
Goal SavingsSomething specific you're working towardsHouse deposit, sabbatical fund, holiday, career breakLow
Stable GrowthSteady, low-risk growth over timeT-bills, fixed deposits, bond fundsLow–Med
High GrowthBigger potential return; you can afford to lose thisStocks, equity funds, high-yield instrumentsHigher
The Sabbatical Fund: A career break is not an accident. It is a decision you fund in advance. Put it in Goal Savings by name. Give it a target amount. Give it a date. That is when it becomes real.

Start with Goal Savings and Stable Growth. Add High Growth when you're ready.
03

TOTAL EXPENSES
SURPLUS / GAP
Income minus Expenses
Annual surprises are not surprises if you plan for them. Divide the annual amount by 12 and add it to your monthly Essentials Third.
Starting later? The Three Thirds still works. If you feel you need to catch up, you may want to increase what you allocate to future-you — even if that means trimming the Security Third for a season. The habit of paying yourself first matters more than the percentage. Start where you are.
04
AUTOMATE EVERYTHING
Set up automatic transfers on payday — before you see the money.
Payday→ Savings transfer fires automatically
Payday→ Investment contribution fires automatically
What remains→ That is your spending money. Work within it.
